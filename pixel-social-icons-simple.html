Instructions for Creating Pixel Art Social Icons

To create pixel art social icons that match your website's theme:

  1. Use a pixel art editor like Piskel, Aseprite, or even MS Paint with zoom
  2. Create 24x24 pixel icons with a transparent background
  3. Keep the design simple and recognizable
  4. Use only white pixels for a clean, monochrome look
  5. Save as PNG files with transparency
  6. Name them: twitter.png, telegram.png, discord.png, medium.png
  7. Place them in the assets/images/social/ directory
